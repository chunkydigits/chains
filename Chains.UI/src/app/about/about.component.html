About the company and the process involved in being part of chains!

The company is a start up with a few goals

These goals are:

  • Reduce delays in housing chains because of miscommunication
  • Let consumers see what the progress is and if something is holding up their house sale
  • Reduce incompetence in the housing market (solicitors and estate agents that are not doing that they need to in a reasonable time)
  • Provide feedback on solicitors and estate agents to help people to find the good ones.
  • Allow people to view chains that need completing

The process is simple, let solicitors and estate agents seamlessly report back to the whole chain when there is a problem.

The hope is that many people that are all working to a single goal should be able to reduce the issues by actually comunicating. There are so many instances where there is no intention to hold up a sale, however misunderstandings on who is waiting on who and what are all too common.
